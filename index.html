SURVIVAL IN SPACE. EXPLORE, REPAIR AND BUILD

THE STORY

Survival in space, you wake up after a long sleep on a deck of a huge interstellar ship stranded in the vast void of space. Discover what has happened and face the many challenges that this place will offer you.

Repair the damage to the ship to survive, manufacture and investigate each sector to collect information about how to carry out this task, be cautious, we do not know if there is someone or something else on board...

FEATURES

  • Explore your own, vast star system and face the mysterious Cerberus forces
  • Colonize numerous planets and watch them grow from lifeless rocks to central hubs of activity
  • Establish diplomatic relations with other players and dictate your terms for military, mining and trade cooperation
  • Build combat, mining and trade ships and customize them with powerful modules
  • Meet other players and complete time-limited objectives near Red Stars that are going Supernova
  • Organize with other players in Corporations and participate in highly tactical White Stars
  • Send your Battleship to the short lived Blue Stars for intense PvP combat
  • Play at your own pace: Nobody will unfairly steal your resources while you are offline
